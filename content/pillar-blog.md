Cosmetic dentistry improves the appearance of your smile while maintaining natural function.

#### Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a safe and effective way to remove stains caused by tea, coffee, tobacco, or aging. Professional whitening provides faster and longer-lasting results compared to home remedies.

Smile designing uses customized treatment plans to enhance the shape, color, and alignment of teeth. Veneers are thin shells placed on the front of teeth to correct chips, gaps, or discoloration.

### Restorative Dental Treatments

Restorative dentistry focuses on repairing damaged or decayed teeth.

#### Dental Fillings and Crowns

Fillings restore cavities, while crowns cover weakened or broken teeth to protect them and restore normal function.

#### Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment removes infected tissue from inside the tooth, relieving pain and saving the natural tooth. Modern techniques make this procedure comfortable and highly successful.

---

### Pediatric Dental Care

Children require special dental attention to ensure healthy development of teeth and gums.

#### Child-Friendly Dental Treatments

Pediatric dental care focuses on gentle treatment, education, and prevention. Dentists help children develop positive dental habits early in life.

#### Preventive Dental Care for Kids

Preventive treatments such as fluoride application and sealants protect children’s teeth from cavities and decay.

---

### Orthodontic Treatments

Orthodontic care improves tooth alignment and bite, enhancing both appearance and oral function.

#### Braces and Aligners

Braces and clear aligners gradually straighten teeth over time. Dentists help patients choose the most suitable option based on their needs and lifestyle.

#### Bite Correction Solutions

Correcting bite issues helps prevent jaw pain, uneven tooth wear, and speech difficulties.

---

### Dental Implants and Tooth Replacement

Missing teeth affect both appearance and chewing ability. Modern dentistry offers reliable replacement options.

#### Single and Multiple Tooth Implants

Dental implants are permanent solutions that replace missing teeth with natural-looking results. They function like real teeth and help preserve jawbone health.

#### Fixed and Removable Dentures

Dentures are cost-effective options for replacing multiple missing teeth and improving facial structure and confidence.
